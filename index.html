🏠 Home Safety

Remove throw rugs. Install grab bars. Night lights on. Non-slip shoes. Phone always within reach. Use walker EVERY time – even for 2 steps.

💊⚡ Watch Out!

Some medicines don't mix: blood thinners + aspirin, water pills + antibiotics, diabetes meds + steroids. Always tell every doctor ALL the medicines you take. Call Carebridge if unsure: (248) 559-5661
